HotelBuddy Guest App Details

The HotelBuddy Guest App provides travelers with a seamless booking experience. Guests can browse available rooms, view high-quality photos, check real-time availability, and make instant reservations. The app supports multiple payment methods including credit cards, digital wallets, and mobile payments.

After booking, guests receive automated confirmations and can manage their reservations directly in the app. Features include itinerary management, check-in/out times, special requests, and local area guides. Push notifications keep guests informed about their upcoming stays and special offers.

The app is available in multiple languages and includes accessibility features to accommodate all travelers. With a clean, intuitive interface, guests can complete bookings in just a few taps.

HotelBuddy Admin App Details

The HotelBuddy Admin App gives hotel managers complete control over their property operations. The dashboard provides an at-a-glance view of key metrics including occupancy rates, revenue, and guest satisfaction scores. Real-time updates ensure staff always have the latest information.

Powerful analytics tools help identify trends and opportunities. Generate custom reports on demand or schedule automated deliveries. The staff management module simplifies scheduling, task assignment, and performance tracking.

Inventory management features allow for dynamic pricing adjustments based on demand. Integrations with popular channel managers ensure your availability is always up-to-date across all booking platforms. The app also includes tools for managing housekeeping, maintenance, and guest communications.
